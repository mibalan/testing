Red

Symbol of guilt, sin, passion and anger. Also used as a symbol of courage and sacrifice.
Used to indicate danger and emergency. Most communists movement were associated with this color.
In yoga it symbolizes the root chakra.

Orange

The national color of the Netherlands. Also the most sacred color in Hinduism.
Associated with joy and sunshine; represents enthusiasm, creativity, success.
In yoga it symbolizes the spleen chakra.

Yellow

Commonly associated with jaundice and cowardice, it can also symbolize friendship, while in heraldry it's the color of honour and loyalty. In politics, usually associated with liberal parties.
In yoga it symbolizes the solar plexus chakra.

Green

Traditional symbol for nature and life, it is also associated with death, sickness and decay.
It's also the traditional color of Islam. In politics, usually associated with nationalist parties.
In yoga it symbolizes the heart chakra.

Blue

Often associated with depth and stability, symbolizes wisdon, intelligence and faith, among others. Commonly used to symbolize the male gender.
In yoga it symbolizes the throat chakra.

Indigo

Bordering between blue and violet, it is a symbol of spiritual realization, solitude and inner communication.
It is said to deepen depression, because of it's inward focus.
In yoga it symbolizes the brow chakra.

Violet

Symbol of luxury, royalty and majesty, it also symbolizes harmony in Chinese culture. Often associated with independence and magic.
In yoga it symbolizes the crown chakra.
